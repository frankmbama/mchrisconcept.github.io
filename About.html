About Us

M-chris Concept is Dedicated to Designing and Construction of Affordable and Aestheic Urban Housing

We are a team of architects, urban planners and builders with extensive experience in the asbuilt and construction industry, accumulating over 5years of professional practice.

Our primary objective is to create affordable and visually appealing urban housing solutions that not only meet the practical needs of residents but also enhance the overall aesthetic quality of urban environments. With a commitmnet to cost-effective design we aim to contribute to the development of sustainable and inclusive communities.
